Fundraising

FUNDRAISING VOLUNTEER

Goal: To assist in successful operations of Vision For Vulnerable Communities Foundation through fundraising.

Objectives of the program

  •     To improve the status and well-being of vulnerable children through support and care in order for them to access essential needs such as education, shelter, clothes, food and medical care.
  •     To provide counseling and recreational activities for vulnerable children.
  •     To provide home and school visits for HIV-positive individuals and other vulnerable households in order to monitor clients progress.
  •     To raise awareness to vulnerable individuals regarding issues of reproductive health, human rights, life skills, and behavioral changes.

Major Responsibility:

  1.     To assist in development and implementation of a coordinated fundraising program.
  2.     Planning for and managing of fundraising events.
  3.     Serves as an ambassador for the organization and promote a positive image.
  4.     Development of donor materials, presentations and mailings.
  5.     Identify donor prospects and research donor prospects.
  6.     Interacting with donors and donor meetings.
  7.     Handling donor acknowledgements and thank you letters.
  8.     Data entry and maintenance of the data base.
  9.     Solicitation of corporate sponsors, financial gifts, and gift-in-kind donations.
  10.     Provide social support to HIV-affected households, orphans and other vulnerable populations.
  11.     Provide school and home-based counseling.
  12.     Educate in nutrition, hygiene, sanitation, and other life skills.
  13.     Prepare workshops and presentations focusing on health, rights, drug and substance abuse, and other issues affecting the communities.
  14.     Conduct administrative work relating to organization development and programming.
  15.     Participate in organizing recreational activities, and retreats for children.
  16.     Provide training to individuals in basic life skills, reproductive health, early pregnancy and relationship.
  17.     Identify ways for fundraising for the programs and to link the organization to international partners.
  18.     Promote the organization by means of articles, photos and other media sources.

Skills & Requirements

  •     Passionate about making a real difference in the lives of others
  •     Dedicated to bettering yourself every day
  •     Inspirational
  •     Results driven
  •     Innovative and creative
  •     Inspired by collaboration
  •     Strong organizational skills
  •     Strong leadership skills
  •     Fundraising experience
  •     Exceptional communication skills
  •     Solution-oriented
  •     A high level of self-confidence
  •     A risk taker
  •     Travel experience (an asset)
  •     First Aid certified (not mandatory at time of hire)
  •     Team or group leadership experience (an asset)
  •     Participation in a past C.L.O.U.D. project or CLOUD Connection (an asset, but not required).
  •     Valid Passport (required)

Benefits:

  3.     Learning new skills.
  4.     Boosting own CV.
