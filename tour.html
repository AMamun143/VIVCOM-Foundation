Slum tour

Slum tour Kampala


Tour for a cause

We shall visit crowded homes, slum trenches, and valleys, red lights districts, historical sites, infrastructures and market places, religious monuments, flashy hotels, national treasures, community schools, Kampala behind the scenes, swamps, working centers, orphanages, slum talents and many more.


What your tour contributions does for the organization

  •     Paying school fees to orphans and vulnerable children
  •     Buying basic needs like food, clothing, beddings, scholastic materials for the children
  •     Paying rent for the orphanage and the school premises
  •     Paying rent and bills for VIVCOM Foundation offices and orphanage premises
  •     Creating income generating activities for the marginalized groups
